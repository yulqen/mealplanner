# Product Guide: Meal Planner

## Initial Concept
A family meal planning application built with Django, HTMX, and Tailwind CSS, focused on organizing weekly meals and transitioning into data-driven nutritional decision-making.

## Vision
To empower families to not only plan their meals but to understand the nutritional impact of their choices. By converting ingredients into actionable data, the Meal Planner helps users set goals, track intake, and make healthier decisions effortlessly.

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Family members looking to organize weekly meals and reduce food waste.
- **Goal-Oriented:** Users who want to use nutritional data to make better food choices and track specific health metrics.

## Key Features
- **Core Meal Planning:** Existing functionality for recipe management, weekly planning, and shopping list generation.
- **Nutritional Integration:** Connect with external databases (USDA, OpenFoodFacts) to automatically fetch nutritional data for ingredients.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Minimalist visualizations of daily and weekly intake.
- **Decision Support:** "Health Score" or grades for planned weeks to encourage better choices before shopping.
- **Metric Tracking:** Support for caloric control and micronutrient tracking (e.g., Sodium, Sugar, Fiber).

## User Experience Goals
- **Minimalist & Functional:** A clean, fast interface where data is helpful but never overwhelming.
- **Unobtrusive Data:** Focus on speed of entry, with nutritional insights provided naturally within the planning workflow.
